How to play

Mobile devices are not yet supported, you will need a mouse (with left- and right-clicking enabled) to play this game.

Welcome to Minesweeper! This classic puzzle game is all about skill, strategy, and a little bit of luck.

Objective

Uncover all the safe squares on the grid without hitting a mine. The numbers you reveal indicate the number of mines in adjacent squares.

Rules

  • Grid: Minesweeper is played on a grid of squares. Left-click on a square to uncover it.
  • Mines: Some squares contain hidden mines. If you uncover a mine, the game ends.
  • Flags: To safely mark a mine without uncovering it, right-click on the square to place a flag.
  • 2
    Numbers: When you uncover a square without a mine, you'll see a number. This number tells you how many mines are in adjacent squares (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).

Strategy

  • Use the numbers to your advantage. If a square has a "1" next to it, there's only one mine nearby. If it has a "2," two mines, and so on.
  • Mark mines with a flag. Right-click to flag squares you suspect have mines.
  • Be cautious when guessing. Sometimes, you'll need to make an educated guess. Just be sure it's your best option.

Winning

To win, uncover all safe squares without hitting a mine, and flag all squares that contain a mine. It's a game of wits, so stay sharp!
